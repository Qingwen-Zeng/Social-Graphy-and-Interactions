'''''Cactus Choir''''' is the first solo album by British keyboardist [[Dave Greenslade]], released in 1976 soon after the disbandment of his own eponymous band, [[Greenslade]].<ref name="allmusic" /> His Greenslade bandmate [[Tony Reeves]] also plays on half of the album's tracks. The artwork for the cover is by [[Roger Dean (artist)|Roger Dean]].

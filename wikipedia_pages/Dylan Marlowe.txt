'''Dylan Marlowe''' is an American [[country music]] singer-songwriter from [[Statesboro, Georgia]] signed to [[Sony Music Nashville]].<ref name="Cantrell-2023" />

==Background==
Marlowe relocated to [[Nashville, Tennessee]] shortly before the start of the [[COVID-19 pandemic]] and signed a publishing and artist development deal with Play It Again Entertainment in 2020.<ref name="Cantrell-2023" /> Following several self-released songs, a growing social media presence, and scoring his first Number One hit as a songwriter with [[Jon Pardi]]'s "[[Last Night Lonely]]",<ref name="Mower-2024">{{Cite web |first=Maxim |last=Mower |date=January 10, 2024 |title=Exclusive: Dylan Marlowe on Authenticity, His Debut Album, 'Boys Back Home' and More |url=https://holler.country/news/breaking/exclusive-dylan-marlowe-on-authenticity-his-debut-album-boys-back-home-and-more/ |access-date=August 9, 2024 |website=Holler}}</ref> it was announced in January 2023, that Marlowe had signed a recording contract with [[Sony Music Nashville]].<ref name="Cantrell-2023">{{Cite web |last=Cantrell |first=LB |date=January 27, 2023 |title=Sony Music Nashville Signs Dylan Marlowe |url=https://musicrow.com/2023/01/sony-music-nashville-signs-dylan-marlowe/ |access-date=August 9, 2024 |website=Music Row}}</ref> His debut EP with the label, ''Dirt Road When I Die'', was released on July 7, 2023.<ref name="EP" /> Marlowe's debut single to country radio, "Boys Back Home" (a duet with [[Dylan Scott]]), was released on December 4, 2023, and ascending the country music top 30. <ref name="Aircheck">{{Cite magazine |date=December 4, 2023 |title=Chart Chat |url=https://assets.countryaircheck.com/public/9a096aad-e54d-4456-8d6f260485724392/Issue-887-December-4-2023.pdf |magazine=Country Aircheck Weekly |issue=887 |accessdate=August 9, 2024}}</ref> Originally recorded in 2021, its release was delayed due to Scott's ''[[Livin' My Best Life]]'', and Marlowe had to later re-record his vocals when the label decided to issue it as his debut single instead of "Record High".<ref name="Mower-2024" /> Marlowe's debut studio album, ''Mid-Twenties Crisis'', was released on September 27, 2024. The album consists of 15 songs including “Heaven’s Sake,” “Boys Back Home,” and the country boy anthem, “Deer on the Wall.”<ref name="Hahnen-2024">{{Cite web |last=Hahnen |first=Madison |date=July 26, 2024 |title=Dylan Marlowe Will Explore Mid-Twenties Crisis On Debut Album |url=https://musicrow.com/2024/07/dylan-marlowe-will-explore-mid-twenties-crisis-on-debut-album/ |access-date=August 9, 2024 |website=Music Row}}</ref>
